Our Introduction

Our enterprising odyssey begin way back in 1988 on Gordon CollegeRoad, Rawalpindi Under the name“Savour” our products not only thepalates but also the hearts of our customers and on 7th August 2001, weinaugurated our new branch in the Melody Foods Market Islamabad. Ourconfidence propelled to new heights as our daughter branch proud to be aresounding success and 24th March 2005 saw the sign board of “Savour” hoisted in the Blue Area Islamabad.In 2010 this new brand was introduced in the Gordon College Road Branch.
