Some History About Us

Our enterprising odyssey begin way back in 1988 on Gordon CollegeRoad, Rawalpindi Under the name “Savour” our products not only thepalates but also the hearts of our customers and on 7th August 2001, weinaugurated our new branch in the Melody Foods Market Islamabad. Ourconfidence propelled to new heights as our daughter branch proud to be aresounding success and 24th March 2005 saw the sign board of “Savour” hoisted in the Blue Area Islamabad. The same year we celebrated the nation’s Independence Day by inaugurating yet another branch IN THE RAWALPINDI CRICKET STADIUM FOOD MARKETS. It was in this Branch that we launched our new brand “Savour Krispo” in 2008. In 2010 this new brand was introduced in the Gordon College Road Branch. We inaugurated our first branch in Lahore on 31st July, 2017 at Shama Chowk, Lahore. Hopefully, very soon the Lahorites shall experience the mouth-watering tastes of these delights at Lakshami Chowk Lahore as well.
